Tea Eggs

Ingredients

  • Coffee
  • Tea
  • Milk

Cooking Instructions

  1. Season 4 raw & thawed chicken breasts with Salt, Pepper, & Oregano
  2. Add olive oil to large skillet on medium heat. When oil is hot, Add 4 chicken breasts into skillet. Cook until chicken reaches 165F. Take chicken out of pan and set aside.
  3. Into the skillet on medium heat, add butter, garlic, cherry tomatoes. Cook until tomatoes burst. Add spinach. Cook until spinach is wilted (1 min).
  4. Set heat to low. Add in heavy cream & parmesian. Simmer until thickened (3-5 minutes).
  5. Optional: In the meantime, slice the chicken into bit sized pieces. Otherwise leave as full breasts to serve.
  6. Add chicken back into pan, simmer 3-5 minutes
  7. Serve with lemon.
